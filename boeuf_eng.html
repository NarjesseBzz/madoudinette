Beef Mafé


The classic dish from West Africa as soon as 6 months old

  • Halal
  • Organic
  • No additives

Offer to your child a true moment of tasting discoveries with our tailor-made Beef Mafé designed for babies as soon as 6 months old.

Designed to meet young children's needs, our version of the classic West Africa's dish combine a delightful taste with well-balanced nutritional valus and sweet flavours to widen babies' culinary horizon safely.


Ingredients

Made of tender beef, stewed organic vegetables and a sweet peanuts sauce, our Beef Mafé has a smooth texture and delicate smoked tatse.



Benefits

Our Beef Mafé was designed to reflect the richness of african traditional cuisines while meeting the specific needs of young children.

Made with natural and balanced ingredients, this baby food is an amazing supply of nutrients essential for the development of children.


Key info


  • Minimum age recommendation: 6 months old
  • Serving: 200 g
  • Allergen: Peanuts
  • Storage instruction: keep at room temperature up to 24h after opening
  • Preparation guidelines: heat using a water bath or a microwave (without the lid)
