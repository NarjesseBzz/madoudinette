Fish Tieb


A unique culinary experience as soon as 6 months old

  • Halal
  • Organic
  • No additives

Let your little-one try an unique culinary adventure with our Fish Thieboudienne.

Inspired by the famous Senegalese recipe, this tasty meal is designed to meet all the nutritionnal needs of young children as as soon as 6 months old.


Ingredients

Our FIsh Thieboudienne is made of healthy and natural ingredients: fonio, vegetables chosen carefully and gently crumbled fish.

Its smooth and melting texture makes it easy to eat for babies. Each bite stimulates the awakening of the senses and the discovery of Africa's flavours.



Benefits

More than a simple dish, our Fish Tieb is a full meal designed to actively help baby's harmonious developement. With its high levels of vitamins and minerals, it helps the growth, the immune system and the proper functionning of the brain all at once.


Key info


  • Minimum age recommendation: 6 months old
  • Serving: 200 g
  • Allergen: Fish
  • Storage instruction: keep at room temperature up to 24h after opening
  • Preparation guidelines: heat using a water bath or a microwave (without the lid)
