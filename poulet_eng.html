Chiken Yassa


The famous Senegalese dish as sonn as 6 months old

  • Halal
  • Organic
  • No additives

Our balanced and mild version of the famous Senegalese dish will alloww your children to discover the flavours of Chicken Yassa as soon as 6 months old while meeting all their nutritional needs.


Ingredients

Our Chicken Yassa is made of tender chicken breast with careffuly stewed vegetables and a sweetened onions sauce appropriate for the delicate taste of little-ones.

Thanks to its creamy texture and the balances flavours, this is the perfect way to introduce babies to rich, varied and cultural tastes.



The benefits

Besides its delightful tatse, Chicken Yassa is a full meal with hight nutrionional values. Each portion has been concieved to support baby's physical and cognitive development everyday.


Key info


  • Minimum age recommendation: 6 months old
  • Serving: 200 g
  • Allergen: none
  • Storage instruction: keep in the fridge up to 24h after opening
  • Preparation guidelines: heat using a water bath or a microwave (without the lid)
